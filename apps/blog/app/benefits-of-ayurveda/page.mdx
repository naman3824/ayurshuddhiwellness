# Benefits of Ayurveda

Ayurveda, the **"Science of Life"**, is one of the world's oldest holistic healing systems. Developed more than 5,000 years ago in India, it is based on the belief that health and wellness depend on a delicate balance between the mind, body, and spirit.

---

## Why Ayurveda?

Unlike modern medicine which often focuses on treating symptoms, Ayurveda aims to find and address the **root cause** of illness. Here are some key benefits:

- **Personalised Treatment** — Every individual has a unique constitution (*Prakriti*), and treatments are tailored accordingly.
- **Natural & Non-Invasive** — Therapies use herbs, oils, dietary changes, and lifestyle adjustments.
- **Preventive Care** — Emphasis on daily routines (*Dinacharya*) and seasonal routines (*Ritucharya*) to prevent disease before it starts.
- **Holistic Approach** — Treats the whole person: physical, mental, and spiritual well-being.
- **Stress Reduction** — Practices like Panchakarma, Yoga, and meditation significantly reduce chronic stress.
